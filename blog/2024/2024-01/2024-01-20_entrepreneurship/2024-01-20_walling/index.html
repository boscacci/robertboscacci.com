Notes on Walling's "Start Small, Stay-Small"

“Start Small, Stay Small” is a 2010 book by Rob Walling. To me it reads like The 4-Hour Workweek meets The Lean Startup. While it’s ancient history by tech standards at 14 years old in 2024, I still found it insightful, compact, and encouraging, so I thought I’d share my re-organized kindle notes here.
