I’m a Seattle-based data engineer, husband, author, and former film colorist / dailies technician.

I currently work with SilverRide to build mission-driven mobility technology for older adults and people who need extra assistance getting where they need to go. In my spare time I enjoy strumming the guitar, cooking for my wife and friends, and playing with my cats.

Lately, I’ve built heaps of automated solutions on Azure and AWS with agentic coding tools like OpenAI’s Codex. I get excited about image analysis and visual question answering tasks.

The Spring of Sight (2022) is my debut nonfiction book. It’s about the ongoing renaissance in Computer Vision and its big implications for society.

Selected Highlights

  • IMDb credits across editorial, camera/electrical, cinematography, editing, visual effects, and sound roles.
